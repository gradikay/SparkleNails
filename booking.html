Nail Care Tips

How to make your manicure last longer

Maintain Your Beautiful Nails

To help your manicure stay perfect for as long as possible, follow these simple aftercare tips:

  • Apply cuticle oil daily to keep your nails and cuticles hydrated.
  • Wear gloves when cleaning or washing dishes to protect your nails from harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using your nails as tools (opening cans, scratching labels, etc.).
  • For gel manicures, apply SPF to your hands before exposure to direct sunlight or UV light.
  • Apply a top coat every 2-3 days to regular polish to extend wear time and add shine.

If you notice any lifting, chipping, or other issues with your nails after your appointment, please contact us within 7 days, and we'll be happy to fix it for you!
