About Us

Our Story

How Sparkle Nails came to be

A Passion for Nail Art

Sparkle Nails was founded in 2020 by Sophia Chen, a passionate nail artist with over 10 years of experience in the beauty industry. What began as a small nail art studio has blossomed into a premier destination for nail care and artistic designs.

Our journey started with a simple vision: to create a salon where clients could express their personality through their nails in a relaxing, luxurious environment. Today, that vision has become a reality as we continue to provide exceptional nail services with the highest standards of quality and creativity.

At Sparkle Nails, we believe that beautiful nails are more than just a beauty treatment—they're a form of self-expression and self-care. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every client leaves our salon feeling confident and beautiful.

Our Salon

A space designed for comfort and relaxation

A Luxury Nail Experience

At Sparkle Nails, we've created a space that feels both luxurious and welcoming. Our salon features comfortable seating, elegant decor, and a calming atmosphere that helps you escape from the outside world during your appointment.

We're equipped with state-of-the-art nail stations, premium pedicure chairs with massage functions, and the latest in nail technology to provide you with the best possible service. Our ventilation system ensures a pleasant environment, and our sterilization protocols exceed industry standards.

Whether you're visiting for a quick polish change or spending a few hours with us for a complete nail makeover, our salon is designed to make your time with us enjoyable and rejuvenating.
